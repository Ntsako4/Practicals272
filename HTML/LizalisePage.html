Biography and Background


Lizalise was born in Durban and went to school in Umlazi. She has always been fascinated by the human body and how it works. She is studying BSc Information and Knowledge systems. Lizalise chose this degree because she wants to design medical devices that can improve healthcare in underserved communities. She is particularly interested in computer and technology.



Hobbies

Lizalise loves reading scientific journals, playing the piano, and volunteering at local clinics. She also enjoys yoga and meditation to stay balanced.
